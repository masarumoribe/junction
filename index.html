Your Favourite Beverages

Enjoy your favourite drinks at the bar counter with the beautiful view of Tokyo's passing trains or at our comfortable lounge. With a wide selection of cocktails, beers, wines and spirits you can ask the bartender to recommend and create a customized drink according to your taste.

Work, Relax, Meet

Our wide selection of coffee and tea makes your afternoon tea dreams come true. Get a cappuccino or latte and you'll be surprised by our barista's beautiful art. Our lounge is also perfect for those who want a beautiful and affordable working space.

Take a break

You can always take a break and play a little ping pong match with your friends. Just order a couple of drinks and while they are ready challenge your friend, the loser pays the bill.
